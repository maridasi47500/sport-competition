✈️ App: Plane Ticket for Sport Competition & Digital Identity

Summary: An app designed to streamline travel planning for athletes attending competitions, while integrating digital identity and social media features.

Bullet Logs:

    🏆 Sport Competition Integration: Syncs with event schedules and locations for easy travel planning.

    ✈️ Plane Ticket Booking: Smart suggestions based on competition dates and athlete preferences.

    🪪 Digital Identity: Secure profile management for athletes, including credentials and affiliations.

    📱 Social Media Sync: Share travel plans, competition updates, and achievements directly to platforms.

    📊 Activity Logs: Track travel history, competition attendance, and performance metrics.

    🔐 Privacy Controls: Manage visibility of identity and social posts with granular settings.
